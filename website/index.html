A notebook app that stays on your computer.

We got tired of this.

Every time you open your notes app, something changed. The sidebar moved. There's a new AI button you didn't ask for. A popup wants you to upgrade. Your own computer became a marketing machine.

We missed the old software. The kind that opened instantly, looked the same every day, and stayed out of your way. Software that felt like a desk — not a storefront.

So we built the app we wanted.

What you get.

  • Notebooks, sections, pages, subpages. Information has hierarchy. Not everything is equally important. Organise it like you think about it.
  • A freeform canvas. Put things where they belong. Drag, resize, arrange. Your screen is a desk, not a feed.
  • Local storage. Your notes live on your machine. No account. No cloud sync. No one else can read them, move them, or delete them.
  • A UI that doesn't change. No surprise redesigns. No feature bloat. Open it tomorrow and it looks exactly like it did today.
